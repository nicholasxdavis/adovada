The Adovada Showdown

FARMesilla presents the inaugural Adovada de Mesilla, a celebration of Southern New Mexico's most iconic dish. This friendly competition brings together the region's best cooks to showcase their unique takes on traditional adovada.

Whether you're a competitor vying for the championship belt or a food lover ready to taste the best adovada in the Southwest, this is the event you won't want to miss!

Competition Rules

General Requirements

  • All adovada must be prepared on-site the day of the event
  • Each competitor must prepare enough for at least 200 tasting portions
  • Traditional New Mexican red chile must be the primary flavor
  • Pork is the required protein (vegetarian options may compete in a separate category)

Judging Criteria

  • Flavor (balance, depth, authenticity) - 40 points
  • Texture and tenderness - 20 points
  • Presentation and creativity - 20 points
  • Adherence to tradition with innovative twist - 20 points
