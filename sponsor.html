Event Overview

ADOVADA de MESILLA celebrates Southern New Mexico's culinary heritage through a friendly adovada competition, community engagement, and cultural preservation.

  • Date: Saturday, May 31, 2025
  • Time: 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Location: FARMesilla, 1840 Avenida de Mesilla, Mesilla, NM 88005
  • Projected Attendance: 100-200+ (food enthusiasts, tourists, NMSU students, retirees)
Event Goals
  • Showcase local culinary talent
  • Foster community spirit
  • Promote Southern NM cuisine
  • Increase FARMesilla awareness
