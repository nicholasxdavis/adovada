Event Rules (Attendees)

To ensure a fair, flavorful, and fun showdown, here’s how the Adovada Smackdown works:

For Chefs

• Must use traditional New Mexican ingredients (red chile is non-negotiable!).
• One signature adovada dish per competitor—no last-minute substitutions!
• Prep on-site or pre-made? Your call, but freshness is key.

Judging Criteria

• Flavor: Depth of chile, balance, and authenticity.
• Texture: Tender pork, sauce consistency.
• Creativity: Unique twists on tradition (without straying too far!).
• Presentation: Feast for the eyes first!

For the Crowd

• Voting: Purchase tokens to cast votes for the People’s Choice Award.
• Samples: Pace yourself—this is a marathon, not a sprint!
• Respect the Heat: Some adovada may bring the fire—water stations provided!

Fair Play

• No bribing judges (tempting, but no).
• Keep it friendly—this is a celebration, not a chili war!
• Winners must graciously accept their title (and bragging rights).

*FARMesilla reserves the right to remove any attendees from the event violating rules. All decisions by staff/judges are final.
